About Greentruth

Greentruth in the Earn Ecosystem

Greentruth is a vertical platform within the Earn ecosystem that provides a market interface and operational layer for verified emissions and environmental data. EarnDLT supplies the underlying infrastructure for verification, tokenization, and registry management.

This arrangement enables Greentruth to deliver a focused, market-ready implementation, built on infrastructure designed for scale and rigor.

Accountability & Controls

Greentruth operates under governance frameworks and operating standards designed for regulated environments. These standards define how the organization manages security, data protection, and regulatory accountability.

These standards ensure Greentruth operates in a manner suitable for audit, assurance, and regulatory use.

  • ISO 27001–aligned information security management practices
  • SOC 2–aligned operational controls governing security, availability, and operational integrity
  • GDPR-compliant data protection and privacy controls across EU operations
  • Regulatory monitoring and alignment processes across operating jurisdictions
