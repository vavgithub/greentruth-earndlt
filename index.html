Caught Between Mandates & Markets

New regulations like California SB 253, EU CBAM, and the EU Methane Regulation—along with frameworks such as OGMP 2.0—now require companies to account for emissions across their entire value chains. Meanwhile, investors, lenders, and insurers demand transparent carbon data to guide capital decisions.

This dual pressure creates two urgent operational challenges:

Meeting your reporting obligations

You're collecting Scope 3 data from hundreds of suppliers—each using different formats, methodologies, and confidence levels—while facing audit risk, compliance deadlines, and mounting disclosure requirements.

Managing emissions strategically

You need credible, granular data to see where emissions exposure sits across your operations and supply chain—and to act on it. Without it, you can't manage risk, target procurement decisions, or demonstrate performance with confidence—to regulators and to markets that reward transparency.

Proof in Action

Tallgrass partnered with Greentruth to integrate verified environmental data directly into its midstream operations. By binding methane and carbon-intensity attributes to real pipeline flows, Tallgrass gained precise, attribute-level visibility into the environmental performance of the gas moving through its system.


The integration strengthens Tallgrass’ ability to serve producers, shippers, and buyers with transparent, audit-ready data—unlocking new value across the ecosystem.

What the integration enables:

  • Attribute-level methane and CI tracking aligned to physical movements
